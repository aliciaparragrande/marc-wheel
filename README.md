# La Ruleta de Marc 🎡

A simple bilingual (Spanish/English) activity wheel, built to help pick one intentional activity to do together — no screens, no scrolling, just spin and go.

## What it does

- Spin the wheel to land on one of 18 starter activities (painting, baking, blanket forts, beach walks, museum visits, storytelling, and more).
- Each activity comes with a short "presence line" — a one-sentence reminder of how to be with him in that moment, not just what to do.
- Filter by category (creative at home, cooking, outdoors, present play, culture) before spinning.
- Switch the whole app between Spanish and English with one tap.
- Add your own activities as you think of new ones.

## How to use it

Just open `index.html` in any browser — no install, no login, no setup. Works on phone or desktop.

## Editing

To change or add activities, open `index.html` and look for the `activities` array near the top of the `<script>` section — each entry has a category, an icon, a name in Spanish/English, and a presence line in Spanish/English.
